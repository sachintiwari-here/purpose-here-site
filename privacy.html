Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Purpose Here ("we", "us") builds Bazaar: Nearby Shops and other products under this studio. This page explains what information is collected and how it's used.

What we collect

  • Basic account details you provide when signing up (phone number, shop details if you're a vendor)
  • Shop and product information entered by vendors using the app
  • Order details placed through the app

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data to third parties
  • We don't show ads or share your information with advertisers

How information is used

Data collected is used only to operate the app — connecting customers with nearby shops, processing orders, and improving the product. As Bazaar is in an early, building-in-public stage, some features (like payments) are still evolving.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, message us directly on WhatsApp.
