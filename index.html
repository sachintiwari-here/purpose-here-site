FAQ

Common questions

Is Bazaar available on the Play Store?+

Not yet. Bazaar is currently available as a direct APK download while we onboard our first shopkeepers and gather feedback. A Play Store release is planned for a later stage.

Is it safe to install the APK?+

Yes. Your phone may show an "unknown source" warning since it's outside the Play Store — this is normal for direct APK installs. You'll need to allow installation from this source once.

How do I list my shop on Bazaar?+

Message us directly on WhatsApp — link above. We're onboarding shopkeepers personally right now, so you'll be talking to a real person, not a form.

Is Bazaar free to use?+

Yes, Bazaar is free for customers to browse and order. We're still finalizing how the shop-owner side will work long-term.

What cities does Bazaar work in?+

Bazaar is starting hyperlocal — currently onboarding shops in Delhi as the first market before expanding further.
