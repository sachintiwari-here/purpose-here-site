PRODUCT 01

Bazaar finds the shop next door, before the algorithm decides which one you see.

BUILDING IN PUBLIC · ONBOARDING FIRST SHOPKEEPERS NOW

Local shopkeepers have no presence beyond their shutter. Bazaar gives them a digital storefront, and gives customers a way to discover what's actually in stock nearby — without scrolling past 40 ads for a shop three cities away.

＋ Dual interface — shop owners manage stock, customers browse and order
＋ Verified-shop tick so customers know who's real
＋ Built and shipped solo, end to end, from a phone
